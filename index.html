About Me

Projects

SEE Tab app

SEETab is an android tablet application used by a team of researchers at ThinkLab (UT Dallas) to study child cognition. This game like research experiment was traditionally conducted using powerpoint, however since that method had practical difficulties and also because children nowadays are tech savvy, the research team decided to conduct such research studies on a tablet.

In this game like application, kids of ages 5-8 will see a set of images(Animals, birds, cars, etc.) and they can choose any one image to seek information based on their curiosity. Right after a short information about that item, children have to rate the quality of the explanation and can opt to know more detailed explanation on that item.

From start to end all the user actions are stored in the database to study the pattern of cognitive behavior. For example, which image was chosen, what was the rating given to the explanation and did the child opt for more information, so on.

Barrel Race

This android game application project aimed to recreate the traditional Barrel Race competition of the famous rodeo event. In this multiplayer game a horse rider attempts to complete a clover-leaf pattern around preset barrels in the fastest time. For maneuvering the horse rider around the clover shaped pattern of barrels, android device’s accelerometer sensor was used such that tilting the device moves the player’s horse in the game.

Easy Review Prototype App

EasyReview is a prototype app that I built to encourage people to change their behavior towards restaurant review/rating system. I observed a behavioral pattern among my friends where each person would look at the reviews before visiting a restaurant but almost never write a review in return after their visit even for extreme good or bad reviews which would benefit others.

With an aim to motivate everyone contribute to the system and initial need finding sessions, I found out that posting an experience as a review about the restaurant takes effort from the user and demotivates them even if they have a valuable review. So reducing the effort needed to post a review was imperative.

On that account, I designed an easy review interface that encourages every one to write the review in a much easier and quicker way.
